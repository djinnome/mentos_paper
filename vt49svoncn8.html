There is an important relationship between the net rate \( \Delta r=r_+ - r_-\) and the net thermodynamic force.  Recall the thermodyamic force in the forward direction is the ln \(L_+=r_+/r_-\), and the likelihood that it will fire in the backward direction is \(L_- = r_-/r_+\)
Then the net likelihood \(\Delta L = L_+ - L_-\)
